# MATLAB Renewable Energy Optimization

This repository contains MATLAB implementations and optimization exercises related to renewable energy systems, based on the **Optimization of Renewable Energy Systems** course. The work focuses on modeling and optimizing the operation of PV systems, battery storage, and grid interaction using linear programming and economic dispatch techniques.
